## Personalization Checklist:
- [ ] Research the website and specific resource page
- [ ] Find the right contact person (often listed on about/contact pages)
- [ ] Mention specific resources already on their page
- [ ] Customize industry terminology
- [ ] Include relevant statistics or case studies
- [ ] Provide clear value proposition
- [ ] Make it easy to say yes (provide direct links)
- [ ] Keep initial email under 150 words
