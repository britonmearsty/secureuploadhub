## Risk Mitigation
- Focus on quality over quantity
- Avoid link farms and PBNs
- Maintain natural anchor text distribution
- Build relationships, not just links
- Monitor for negative SEO attacks

## Expected Timeline for Results
- **0-3 months**: Foundation building, first 10-20 quality backlinks
- **3-6 months**: 50+ backlinks, improved domain authority
- **6-12 months**: 100+ backlinks, significant organic traffic growth

Remember: Link building is a marathon, not a sprint. Focus on creating genuine value for your target audience, and the links will follow naturally.
